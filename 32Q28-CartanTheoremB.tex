\begin{thm}[Cartan]
Suppose $\mathcal{F}$ is a coherent analytic sheaf on a Stein
manifold $M$.
Then for $k > 0$, then 
\begin{equation*}
H^k(M,\mathcal{F}) = 0 .
\end{equation*}
\end{thm}

Here, $H^k(M,\mathcal{F})$ is the $k$th cohomology group valued in the sheaf
$\mathcal{F}$
