Kontinuitätssatz


Theorem.

G⊂ℂn is pseudoconvex if and only if for any family of closed analytic discs {dα}α∈I in G with ∪α∈I∂⁡dα being a relatively compact set in G then ∪α∈Idα is also a relatively compact set in G.

This is the analogue of one of the definitions of a convex set. Just replace pseudoconvex with convex and closed analytic discs with closed line segments.
